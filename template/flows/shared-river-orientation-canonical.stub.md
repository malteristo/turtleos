# Shared river orientation — canonical FAQ

Distill space-specific answers here as dogfood surfaces gaps. Until expanded, these defaults apply.

## Privacy firewall

- Private rivers stay private. Nothing from someone's personal channel appears here automatically.
- Being in this shared channel does not expose your private eddies to other members.

## Sharing into this space

- Share is **opt-in**: `!share` in an eddy → pick this space → confirm.
- A short digest appears in the parent channel; a shared eddy opens for space members.
- The sharer is not always in the shared eddy until they choose to join.
- Guest practitioners may share **to** this space when share policy allows — without joining this Discord channel.

## Who is a space member

- Registry members auto-join shared eddies opened here.
- Discord channel membership and share picker eligibility can differ — ask if something feels wrong.

## Re-share outward

- Sharing from a shared eddy to someone's private river posts a transparency note in this parent channel.
