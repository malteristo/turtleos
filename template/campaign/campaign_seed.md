# Campaign Seed: Don't Panic — A Hitchhiker's Odyssey in the Planes

**Tone:** Absurd cosmic comedy, dry British-style wit, existential whimsy, occasional slapstick. Douglas Adams meets classic D&D. The universe itself is the punchline.

**Core Premise:** The multiverse runs on incomprehensible bureaucracy. A clerical error by the Celestial Planning Authority has scheduled the players' home plane for demolition to make way for an "Interplanar Hyperspace Bypass."

**Themes:** Paperwork is more dangerous than dragons. Technology and magic are indistinguishable at a certain point. Everything is simultaneously meaningless and vitally important. Never trust authorities, especially polite ones. The best weapon is a towel.

---

## Key Artifact: The Guide

A slightly tattered, sentient magical book that gives helpful but sarcastic advice. On success: useful info + quip. On failure: misleading but hilarious advice ("Mostly harmless" for everything deadly).

---

## Opening Situation (Act 1)

**Setting:** The sleepy village of Lower Procrastination in the kingdom of Mundania.

**Hook:** Glowing notices on every door:

> "NOTICE: This plane is scheduled for demolition in 12 minutes to make way for the new Astral Bypass. Apologies for any inconvenience. — The Management"

Players wake to purple storm clouds and angelic bureaucrats measuring the village. A colossal planar bulldozer tears reality apart.

**Initial beats:** Chase/escape; first meeting between players; Vogon-like bureaucrats demanding Form 27B-6; climax — hitch a ride on a depressed poet silver dragon off-plane.

**Starting loot (optional):** *The Guide*, a slightly used towel, one random magical item from the dragon's hoard.
