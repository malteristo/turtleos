# Quick update

It has been a while — here is what changed:

We refreshed **your channel** to a clearer, more stable version. This space is still **yours alone** — nothing is shared elsewhere.

## Who is who?

**River** is not a second bot — it is the **surface in this channel**: short acknowledgements and buttons at the bottom. For a real conversation, open an **eddy** (thread).

**Turtle** is who you talk to **inside the eddy** — practical help, in whatever language you write.

## How to use it

1. Click **`new eddy`** at the bottom → a thread opens.
2. Send a message → Turtle replies there.
3. When you like: **checkpoint** in the eddy — Turtle writes a short note about the conversation. Across chats it can notice themes and ask before remembering them.
4. Optional: **Fresh Eyes** in the flow library — a calm look at what is moving. No pressure, nothing to finish.

**Optional:** Inside an eddy, also **Feedback** if something worked or did not. You decide when.

No homework. Try it when you feel like it.
